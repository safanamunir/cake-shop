About us

A new shop was built in the Sylvanian Village. It was a very grand, beautiful shop with white walls and a pink pointed roof. The children looked in the windows to see what kind of shop it would be. Most of the children thought it would be a jeweller’s shop. But then one day, a delicious smell came from inside. “It’s a cake shop!” cried Freya the Chocolate Rabbit girl. “Hooray!” cried Ralph the Walnut Squirrel boy. The children were very happy about the new shop. The baker of the cake shop was Veronica the Toy Poodle mother. The shelves of the shop were full of beautiful cakes that Veronica had made. She was very particular about both the ingredients and the design, and the cakes looked they were covered with jewels. “I’m going to make a special cake to celebrate the opening of this shop”,said Veronica. “Would you like to help?” The children were very excited. “We’re going to make the most beautiful cake ever!” they said.
